This protocol suite was developed under... unusual conditions.

|------|-------------|
| **Principal Investigator** | Has zero prior exposure to target compounds (has never tasted Korean food) |

Rather than acquiring prepared compounds from commercial sources (i.e., restaurants), this project documents synthesis procedures for operator self-sufficiency.

## Why This Format?

The target operator's professional background is in medical laboratory sciences. Standard recipe formats cause decision paralysis. This format:

- **Leverages existing cognitive frameworks** â€” She already follows SOPs at work
- **Eliminates ambiguous terminology** â€” No "season to taste" or "cook until done"
- **Provides quantified measurements** â€” Specific volumes and times
- **Includes QC checkpoints** â€” Visual/tactile verification at critical stages
- **Reduces paralysis in fatigued states** â€” Just follow the steps

> [!note] The Core Insight
> Cooking is just a laboratory protocol with edible outputs. If you can run a cell count, you can run a stove.
> 
> The terminology is different, but the logic is identical:
> - Follow the procedure
> - Monitor the reaction
> - Hit your checkpoints
> - Log anomalies
