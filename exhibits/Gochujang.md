## Capsaicin Safety

> [!caution] Handling Notes
> Gochujang contains capsaicin (the compound that makes chili peppers spicy).
> 
> - **Avoid touching your eyes** after handling
> - **Wash hands** after use
> - If you eat too much: dairy (milk, yogurt) neutralizes capsaicin better than water
> 
> That said, gochujang is mild enough that casual handling isn't a problem. Just don't rub your eyes.
